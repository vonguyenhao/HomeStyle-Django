Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HomeStyle’s furniture, delivery, services, and more. If your question isn’t listed here, feel free to contact us directly!

1. What types of furniture does HomeStyle offer?

We offer a wide variety of furniture for Living Rooms, Bedrooms, Dining Areas, and Bathrooms. From sofas and beds to vanities and cabinets, all our items are curated to fit modern lifestyles and local preferences.

2. Do you deliver to rural areas outside Darwin?

Yes! We deliver across the Northern Territory including some remote areas. Delivery times and fees may vary based on your location.

3. How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days within Darwin. For outer areas, it may take longer. We will confirm estimated delivery when you place your order.

4. Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy to ensure your satisfaction.

  • Return Period: Products can be returned within 14 days of delivery.
  • Condition: Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in resalable condition. All tags, manuals, and accessories must be included.
  • Exclusions: Customized, clearance, and assembled items cannot be returned unless faulty or damaged.
  • Refunds: Once the returned item is inspected and approved, refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Exchanges: You can request an exchange for a different size or color. Exchanges are subject to product availability.
  • Return Shipping: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or sent in error.
  • Damaged or Incorrect Items: If you receive a damaged or wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours for a full replacement or refund.

For assistance with returns or exchanges, please contact our support team via email or use the inquiry form on the Contact Us page.

5. Is Afterpay available for all purchases?

Afterpay is available for orders above $100. Simply select Afterpay at checkout to split your payments into four easy installments.

6. How do I contact customer service?

You can email us via the Contact Us page, chat with us when online, or call us directly during business hours at +61 123 456 789.
